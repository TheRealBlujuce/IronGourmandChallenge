The Iron Gourmand

Current Deaths: 0

The Rules

One Life Only

  • Death is permanent; if your character dies in a particular zone, you cannot quest there again on your next run. In addition, if you die, you must delete your character. You must live off the land and use your professions of choice to help you survive the open world.

Exploring Azeroth

  • New characters must go through Exile's Reach first. Allied Races, on the other hand, must go through their racial zone instead. For Cataclysm, Wrath, and Pandaria, you can use Chromie Time but must stay in Azeroth. Only ground mounts can be used but Dragon Riding is useable once within the Dragon Isles.

The Clothes On Your Back

  • You are only allowed to use gear crafted by yourself and tabards that provide no bonuses to xp or stats. Buffs gained from class abilities and food cooked by yourself are permitted. Additionally, you must play solo and cannot send yourself money, gear, or other items. The AH and Mail box are also prohibited.

Trial By Combat

  • Ensure that Warmode is turned on as soon as possible, and avoid participating in dungeons, raids, world bosses, pet battles, arenas, or battlegrounds. Additionally, refrain from using any experience-boosting activities, gear, or buffs, such as the Darkmoon Faire, Heirlooms, Monk XP Buff, and similar items.

Hearth And Home

  • Any race and class combination is permitted, but Allied Races must sell all starting gear and buy white gear instead. Also, before moving on to the achievements of Classic, Northrend, Cataclysm, Pandaria, BFA, and Dragonflight, Allied Races must complete the cooking and fishing achievements of their racial zone first.
