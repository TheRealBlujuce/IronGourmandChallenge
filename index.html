Iron Gourmand Challenge

Current Deaths: 0

The Rules

One Life Only

  • Death is permanent. If you died in a particular zone, you can not quest there again on your next run. You must live off the land. Hunt, Skin, Fish, Sew and Craft. Use your proffesions of choice to help you survive the open world.

Exploring Azeroth

  • Must go through Exiles Reach if not using an allied race. Allied Races must go through their racial zone first. Chromie Time for Cataclysm, Wrath and Pandaria can be used. You must stay in Azeroth. No Flying Mounts can be used. Dragon Riding however, can be used once in the Dragon Isles.

The Clothes On Your Back

  • Only gear you craft yourself can be used. Tabards that do not give any boosts to xp or stats are ok. You must also play Solo. You can not send yourself money, gear or other items. The AH and Mail is also prohibited. Buffs gained from your class abilities or food you cooked yourself is allowed.

Trial By Combat

  • Warmode must be used and turned on as soon as possible. Dungeons, raids, world bosses, pet battles, arenas or battlegrounds are not allowed. Experience boosting activites, gear or buffs are not alowed. This includes but is not limited to: The Darkmoon Faire, Heirlooms, Monk XP Buff, etc.

Hearth And Home

  • Any race and class combo can be used. However, Allied Races must vendor all gear and purchase white starting gear. Allied Races must complete the cooking/fishing achievements of their racial zone first before moving on to the classic, northrend, cataclysm, pandaria, bfa, and dragonflight achievements.

The Goals

Complete The Following Achievements And Reach Level 70

The Old World

  • Lunch Lady
  • Short Order Cook

Northrend

  • Northrend Cook
  • Northrend Fisherman
  • The Northrend Gourmet

Cataclysm

  • Cataclysmic Cook
  • Cataclysmic Fisherman
  • The Cataclysmic Gourmet

Pandaria

  • Zen Master Fisherman

Zandalar / Kul'Tiras

  • Zandalari/Kultiran Cook
  • Zandalari/Kultiran Fisherman
