The Rules

  • Death = Delete.
  • Death is permanent. If you died in a particular zone, you can not quest there again on your next run.
  • Starting Zone
  • Must go through Exiles Reach if not using an allied race. Allied Races must go through their racial zone first.
  • Gear
  • Only gear you craft yourself can be used. Tabards that do not give any boosts to xp or stats are ok.
  • Assistance
  • You must play Solo. No Sending yourself money, gear or other items. The AH and Mail is also prohibited.
  • Living off the Land
  • You must live off the land. Hunt, Skin, Fish, Sew and Craft away. Use the proffesions to help you survive Azeroth.
  • No Boosts
  • XP-boosting activites, gear or buffs are not alowed. This includes but is not limited to: Darkmoon Faire, Heirlooms.
  • Self Help Abilities
  • Only buffs gained from your class abilities, casted on you by other players or food is allowed.
  • Trial By Combat
  • Warmode must be used. Dungeons, raids, world bosses, pet battles, arenas or battlegrounds are not allowed.
  • Azeroth Needs You
  • You can not leave Azeroth.Chromie Time for Cataclysm, Wrath and Pandaria can be used.
  • Explore
  • No Flying Mounts can be used. Dragon Riding however, can be used once in the Dragon Isles.
  • The Races of Azeroth
  • Only the races and classes below can be used. Allied Races must vendor all gear and purchase white starting gear.
  • Hearth Is Home
  • Allied Races must complete the cooking/fishing achievements of their racial zone first before moving on to the others.
